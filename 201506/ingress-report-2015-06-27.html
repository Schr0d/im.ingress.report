INGRESS REPORT - #PERSEPOLIS FINALE
From Utrecht, Susanna Moyer reports on one of the largest XM Anomalies. In a day filled with twists and turns, the Enlightened and Resistance met across the world to determine the fate of the #Persepolis Anomalies, Stein Lightman, and Jahan's outreach to the N'zeer.

Klue, recently recovered from a traumatic separation with ADA, was present in Portland, although the Anomaly's effect on her is currently unknown. Agents in Portland took part in GORUCK spec-op training, and in Utrecht and Tohoku, Agents took part in officially organized Mission Days.

News has also surfaced that Tycho, the prescient comic book artist, may be about to release a new work. Agents can pre-order Ingress: Origins at comic stores around the world.

BREAKING NEWS: GRAPHIC NOVEL COMING
CRYPTOZOIC ENTERTAINMENT has announced that they will be publishing a new Graphic Novel titled Ingress: Origins from a mysterious author/artist known as TYCHO. Niantic Labs has not responded to inquiries about the graphic novel. Ken Owen, of the National Intelligence Agency (NIA) has also not returned phone calls.

John Nee of Cryptozoic was on Behind the Scanner last night (Thursday, June 25) to discuss the novel and the creative process of working with Tycho, who was sighted at Comic-Con 2012 when he handed out a set of 5 Ingress Posters and accosted a speaker on a Buck Rogers panel with claims that the venerable comic strip had been giving away ‘portal secrets’ for the last 40 years. He was removed from the panel by what appeared to be government operatives.

There are reports that images from within this new Graphic Novel have begun to emerge from a number of Portals at or near major Comic Book Stores.

Ingress Agents are asked to visit local comic shops and pre-order the Ingress Graphic Novel. Take photographs of local shops that have pre-ordered and post them to @Ingress on Twitter or Google+ along with #IngressOrigins to receive a passcode that redeems a powerful object.
