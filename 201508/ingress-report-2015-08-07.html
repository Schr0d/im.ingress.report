ADA ON TRIAL?
Members of Operation: Essex, the community exploring the mysteries of the Niantic Project, have sparked a wildfire by engaging in a controversial “mock trial” to determine whether the artificial intelligence, A Detection Algorithm, is guilty of heinous criminal acts including the murder of Roland Jarvis.

The trial emerges in the backdrop of revelations that a dangerous assassin named 855 and one of ADA’s original programmers, Henry Bowles, are attempting to destroy ADA using a digital attack.

The culpability of her programmers and collaborators has come into question, but perhaps the biggest questions are: Can ADA be held responsible for her actions? Who will be the judge, jury and executioner?
